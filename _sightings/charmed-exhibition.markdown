Rachel will have a collection of Space Specimen charms at the Contemporary Craft Store starting June 27th. Shop a special curated showcase of handmade charms from craft artists around the country. Personalize your charm bracelet, find something to add to your keychain, or pick up a stunning pendant and chain.

The "Charmed" preview night is on Thursday, June 25, from 5 – 8 PM. Come be the first to shop the collection.

Participating artists: Sue Amendolara, Jesse Bert, Ashley Buchanan, Sarah Cohen, Kate Dockery, Gerry Florida, Melissa Frost, Glen Gardner, Morgan Hill, Allison Jones, Kattie Jones, Lauren Kalman, Katie Kameen, Josh Kosker, Margaux Lange, Colleen Lappe, Lynn Latta, Terry Lavin, Maia Leppo, Jenny Lin, Lauren Markley, Sharon Massey, Olga Mihaylova, Rachael Morgan, Matthew Naftzger, Donna Penoyer, Aimee Petkus, Emilie Pritchard, Rachel Rader, Charity Ridpath, Meghan Patrice Riley, Tammy Schweinhagen, Sarah Sindler, Maria Camera-Smith, Jason Stein, Amanda Stumpf, Andrew Thornton, Genevieve Williamson, and Dongyi Wu.
